The Area

Hvar at Its Best

The villa sits above the action — close enough to walk into Hvar Town for dinner, far enough that you never hear it. The Adriatic is visible from every room.

01
Hvar Town

One of the most beautiful towns in the Mediterranean. Renaissance squares, Venetian architecture, excellent restaurants and a harbour that fills with superyachts in peak season.

10 min drive
02
Pakleni Islands

A chain of pine-covered islands just offshore — clear water, secluded coves, and waterfront restaurants you can only reach by boat. Worth every minute.

15 min by boat
03
Stari Grad

The quieter, older side of Hvar island — a 2,400-year-old town with no traffic, a beautiful bay, and some of the best wine in Croatia coming from the surrounding plains.

25 min drive
04
Bonj Les Bains

Croatia's most celebrated beach club — a natural pebble cove with white umbrellas, a cocktail bar, and water so clear you can see the bottom at five metres. Book in advance.

20 min drive
